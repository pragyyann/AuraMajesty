# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- **Salon Clients**: Individuals looking to discover services, view stylist portfolios, and easily book premium appointments.
- **Potential Clients**: Visitors looking to experience the brand's aesthetic, review transparent pricing, and understand the high-end experience before booking.
- **Salon Staff/Owners**: Secondary users managing appointments, schedules, and client communications.

## Product Purpose
Aura Majesty Studio is a premium digital front door for a modern, high-end salon. It exists to translate the physical luxury, sleekness, and precision of the salon experience into a digital medium, enabling seamless service discovery and high-conversion, low-friction booking.

## Brand Personality
Modern, sleek, and minimalist. Visualized through a sophisticated monochromatic palette, clean and precise lines, spacious layout structures, and high contrast typography. It balances high-fashion minimalism with welcoming warmth to remain approachable yet highly aspirational.

## Anti-references
- **Sterile & Clinical Portals**: Avoid cold, overly-engineered booking interfaces that lack brand character and warmth.
- **Cluttered Templates**: Avoid generic templates utilizing low-quality stock imagery, cramped layouts, and loud/unrefined neon accents.
- **Intimidating Dark UIs**: Avoid dark, moody, and low-contrast designs that feel uninviting or make navigation difficult.

## Design Principles
- **Restrained Opulence**: Luxury is communicated through space, typography, and precise alignment rather than gold foils or excessive decoration.
- **Frictionless Sophistication**: The booking flow must feel as effortless and smooth as the physical services provided.
- **Intimate Minimalist Warmth**: Ensure the clean, minimalist aesthetic feels human, inviting, and warm through subtle micro-interactions, editorial layout structures, and thoughtful copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **W3C WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: Maintain a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for body text and 3:1 for large text.
- **Intuitive Keyboard Navigation**: Ensure booking forms and interactive elements are fully navigable via keyboard.
- **Responsive Layout Integrity**: Ensure content scales beautifully without overflow or loss of function across all device sizes.
